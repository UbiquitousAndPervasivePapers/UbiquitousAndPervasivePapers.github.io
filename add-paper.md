To add a paper to this list:

Write an E-Mail to me (peter.zdankin@uni-due.de) containing a reference to the paper you would like to suggest.


Alternatively: If you want to make my life easier, send the papers with this template and replace the < > tags:
```text
---
layout: paper
title: "<title>"
authors: <Authors et al.>
year: <1995>
area: <The area you would group this in>
venue: <venue>
pdf: <URL>
---

<Abstract (or summarize this in your own words)>
```
